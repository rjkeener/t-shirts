What is this?

The SET High Media T-Shirt Contest and Media Day is a event/activity that involves everyone in SET High School to Create, Buy and Share there T-Shirt online through Social Media. It is a amazing way to show our school to other Students in the Community of San Diego. Students are encouraged to creat a desighn that shows the great parts of our school.

Rules of the Desighn

What the Art Must Include

Must Say SET

There must be the word SET in the Desighn and it must have the School Colors (Marron, Gold & Grey). The Font can be different as well the size, but the name must be visible and clear.

Represent our Community

Remember this is going to show our comunity. So please show the amazing part of our community. For some examples there is the We Have your Back, May Matters, Social Venture Sandbox, and The Ready SET Soar Hour Talk Show.

Freehand or Digital

You can Freedraw it on paper or Use a Digital Version. If you draw it on Paper then the Size must be 11X14. We will not be abile to accept anything less. If you need some paper please see Mr. Heid.

Steps to this Project

Step #1 - Desighn

Students will submit there Artwork to the two Project team members. All artwork will need to fallow the Rules then we will place them into a folder for when we vote.

Rules Submit

Step #2 - Poll

All of the Artwork that has been submited will be placed into a Poll where the students will get to vote on which artwork they would like to have on the T-Shirts.

Step #3 - Sell

We will sell the T-Shirts to the Community of SET High for Students/Guardians to buy. A simple and easy way to get one.

Step #4 - Photo Day

We will anounce the Photo day when students will be abile to share the Photo with them all wearing SET T-Shirts on every posible Social Media Platform. We will Anounce the date soon.
